## Style Prompt

Short, high-energy launch trailer for a Singapore hackathon demo. The message is blunt: creators, artists, and SMEs need money; the Singapore state is the biggest customer; public-sector opportunities are trapped behind GeBIZ monitoring, tender reading, quotation work, and follow-up admin. The video should feel like a night drive through Marina Bay: dark civic-tech canvas, electric scanlines, amber city light, sharp kinetic type, and real product screenshots moving like evidence in a mission-control system. It should be polished, direct, and creator-facing, not a generic productivity pitch.

## Colors

- `#05070D` — night canvas
- `#0B1724` — skyline shadow
- `#00E5FF` — GeBIZ/data scan cyan
- `#FFB000` — Singapore city light amber
- `#FF3D8B` — kinetic accent
- `#3DFF99` — approval green
- `#F5F7FB` — primary copy

## Typography

- Display: `Arial Black`, `Montserrat`, `Helvetica Neue Condensed Black`, sans-serif
- UI and captions: `Inter`, `Helvetica Neue`, Arial, sans-serif

## Motion Rules

- Kinetic text enters with fast y-movement, blur, and scale, then gets out of the way.
- Product screenshots move on rails with scan bars, match chips, and approval overlays.
- Singapore skyline remains present as geography and mood, with subtle parallax only.
- Keep total duration under 15 seconds.

## What NOT to Do

- Do not use generic startup gradients as the main visual.
- Do not fake product screenshots; use real app screenshots where shown.
- Do not make the clip rely on voiceover to understand the core loop.
- Do not publish or submit anything externally from this project without explicit approval.
